Calendula

Also known as pot marigolds, calendulas are bright, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of golden orange and sunshine yellow.

Yellow Bell

In the Philippines, the "yellow bell flower" typically refers to a flowering plant known scientifically as Tecoma stans.

Viola

Viola flowers are small flowers that resemble pansies, but have various colors and they belong to the Viola genus, which contains over 500 species.

Gumamela

Gumamela is the Filipino name for the Hibiscus flower, specifically referring to Hibiscus rosa-sinensis, which is also known as the Chinese hibiscus or shoe flower.
