Deep Affection

Happy 1st year of friendship to you and me. It’s finally here, the day we’ve waited so long. It’s been one year already since we started talking to each other. I never thought that we could reach this far, I’ve never imagined that I could spend a year with you.

Although it isn’t that surprising because, from the moment I interacted with you, I felt something different. It suddenly grows after passing just a few months later, it’s what we call love. Our development is already under the roof, we have improved so much, and we learned from each other through making mistakes.

Just like what happened on the last day, we argued again. But I’d say that it’s healthy because we can’t develop our feelings for each other if we don’t learn to deal with the mistakes we’ve made. No matter what happens, we will continue to move forward, and we should face every problem that may occur in our path.

Also, I apologize for making you wait. A lot of things have happened here, and my mind can’t think straight, but when I talk to you, everything changes. But then again, I must stand up for myself and be strong so that I won’t have to worry. Always remember that I love you so much.

Trust

One of the most important parts of a relationship is to trust one another completely. You must be able to trust that they won’t stray, and you trust them with your feelings.

Vulnerability

Opening to your partner without fear of judgment is amazing. They get to know you better and it can strengthen your relationship. If you don’t allow vulnerability, your relationship will suffer.

Respect

When you respect and love one another, you’ll have a beautiful relationship. This foundation of trust and security is essential and will lead to wonderful things.
